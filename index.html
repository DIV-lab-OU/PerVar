Perceptual Variability in Visualization

Study Details

  • Format: 45-minute online interview
  • Platform: Zoom
  • Participants: Researchers, practitioners, domain experts
  • Recording: Audio with consent
  • Timeline: Interviews start early January

Meet the Researchers

We are studying how individuals interpret the same visualization in different ways, a phenomenon we call perceptual variability. We invite academic researchers, visualization practitioners, and domain experts to share their perspectives in a 30-minute recorded interview conducted online (Zoom/Google Meet). Your insights will help develop conceptual models and practical guidance for studying and designing for interpretive differences. Your participation helps shape practical guidance for research and design by sharing your expertise.
