Perceptual Variability in Visualization

We are studying how individuals interpret the same visualization in different ways—a phenomenon we call as perceptual variability. We invite researchers, visualization practitioners, and domain experts to share their perspectives in a 30-minute recorded interview conducted online (Zoom/Google Meet). Your insights will help develop conceptual model and practical guidance for studying and designing for interpretive differences.

Participate in a 30‑minute interview

Study Details

  • Format: 30-minute online interview
  • Platform: Zoom or Google Meet
  • Participants: Researchers, practitioners, domain experts
  • Recording: Audio/video with consent
  • Timeline: Interviews start early December

Why Participate

  • Contribute expert insight to advance understanding of perceptual variability.
  • Help shape practical guidance for research, evaluation, and design.
  • Share domain perspectives that inform future visualization practice.
